About Us Page





The Information Technocrat's Student Forum is a club formed by the students of the IT department of VIIT. WE MAKE IT HAPPEN.

Codechef Campus Chapter is the subsidary of ITSF which organises Coding Contests on Codechef and webinars on Competative Programming.
